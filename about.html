About Us

From a young man's dream to a community emplyer

Jordan Ways of Cooking restaurant is founded and run by the Head Chef, Ntlalo Jordan. Our establishment has become a staple for the Langa community and the beaming pride and joy. Jordan is a Johannesburg born chef with a passion for fine cuisine, who cherry-picked his favourite dishes from working as a Sous Chef at some of the world’s glamorous hotels and started his fine dining restaurant in Langa township. The restaurant serves a friendly mix of international 5 star gourmet meals and traditional South African food that locals and tourists alike - keep coming back for more. The journey has had its challenges and Jordan continues to soldier on because he believes in the vision of seeing his restaurant elevate the township’s economy. “The drive I have for building my restaurant is because I want to share my love for fine dining with people in my community. As most of them have never stepped out of this province, so from my travels and time abroad - I want to take them on a trip around the world with every course. I believe that food breaks every cultural barrier and it’s the best way to communicate because everyone loves good food. “ - Chef Ntlalo Jordan
